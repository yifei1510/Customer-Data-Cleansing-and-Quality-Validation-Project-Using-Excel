## Executive Summary

Cleaned a raw customer dataset in Excel by resolving duplicate rows, repeated customer IDs, invalid name entries, inconsistent category values, mixed date formats, extra spaces, and missing data.

Used **Remove Duplicates, Find and Replace, Filters, Text to Columns, helper checker columns, and Excel formulas including `TRIM()`, `PROPER()`, and `OR()`** to validate and standardise the data.

Produced a final dataset of **430 cleaned rows and 8 columns**, standardised key fields such as `Gender`, `Marital_Status`, and `Location`, and separated **50 repeated records across 19 customer IDs** for further review. The cleaned dataset is analysis-ready and can be directly imported into tools such as **Power BI, Excel, SQL, and other reporting platforms**.
