## Executive Summary

Cleaned and validated a raw customer dataset in Excel by resolving duplicate rows, repeated customer IDs, invalid name entries, inconsistent category values, mixed date formats, extra spaces, and missing data. Reduced the dataset from **999 raw rows** to **430 cleaned rows and 8 columns**, including the removal of **511 fully blank rows (51.2%)**, while improving overall data consistency and reporting readiness.

Used **Remove Duplicates, Find and Replace, Filters, Text to Columns, helper checker columns, and Excel formulas such as `TRIM()`, `PROPER()`, and `OR()`** to standardise key fields, validate suspicious records, and isolate **50 repeated records across 19 customer IDs** for further review. The final dataset is analysis-ready and can be directly imported into tools such as **Power BI, Excel, SQL, and other reporting platforms**.
